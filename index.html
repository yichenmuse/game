About Right Jump

Game Overview

Right Jump is an exciting casual platformer game that tests your agility and strategy. Time your jumps perfectly to navigate through tricky platforms, elevators, trampolines, and moving obstacles. Each level presents new challenges that will test your skills and timing.

How to Play

  • Hold the left mouse button to aim your move
  • Release to jump
  • Alternatively, use AD or arrow keys
  • Time your jumps perfectly
  • Navigate through various obstacles
